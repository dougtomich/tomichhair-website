Curl Wisdom

Knowledge is
part of the ritual.

In Brazil, curl care is passed down — mother to daughter, friend to friend. Here is some of ours. Free, forever.

Water first

Style your curls while they're soaking wet — not just damp. Product applied to wet hair seals in moisture, and moisture is what keeps frizz away all day.

Scrunch, never rub

Terry towels rough up the cuticle and break your curl pattern. Blot and scrunch upward with a soft t-shirt or microfiber towel instead — your curls will thank you.

Protect the crown

Sleep on satin or silk — a pillowcase, a bonnet, whichever feels like you. Cotton pulls moisture from your hair overnight; satin lets your curls wake up defined.
