Our Story

Built from
love, roots,
and curls.

"She came from Brazil, where curls are celebrated — where the right product is a ritual, not an afterthought."

Tomich Hair is a Houston-based family business built by a husband, a wife, and their son. The brand carries our family name — Tomich — because this is personal.

It started with a wife who knew exactly what her curls needed and couldn't find it here. She inspired everything. She brought with her a deep knowing — the kind that comes from a culture that honors curls, that treats curl care as something sacred.

We built Tomich Hair together, as a family. The name on every product is the name on our front door — because it was always going to be that way.

The Name
